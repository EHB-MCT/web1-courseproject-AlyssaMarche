Harry Potter

I’m Harry Potter, and I spent six transformative years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It's a magical place where I learned not just spells and potions, but also about who I truly am. You’ll find more details about my journey later in my CV, but let me give you a glimpse of what shaped me.

Before Hogwarts, my life was far from ordinary. For twelve years, I lived in a cupboard under the stairs at my aunt and uncle’s house, unaware of the magic within me. My parents died when I was just one year old, and for years, my relatives kept the truth from me. It wasn’t until a mysterious man arrived at our vacation home that I discovered the truth—that my parents hadn’t just died in an accident; they were killed by a dark wizard, my greatest enemy, Voldemort. Learning this was not easy, but it pushed me to grow stronger and face the challenges ahead.
